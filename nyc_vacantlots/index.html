Welcome to NYC
Residential Zoning &
Vacant Lots Map

The map provides a simple way to research vacant lots suitable for residential development and zoning regulations.

Find the zoning for the lots of interest, discover new proposals for your neighborhood, and learn where City Planning initiatives are happening throughout the City.

Zoning Questions

For general zoning questions, read the Zoning Help Desk FAQ. If your question isn't answered there, call 212-720-3291 during business hours (8:30AM–5:30PM, Monday–Friday, closed on legal holidays). Leave a detailed message with your block and lot information, and a zoning specialist will get back to you within two business days. Or fill out the Zoning Information Inquiry Form.
